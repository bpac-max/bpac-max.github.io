Our Home

Citi Field

The House in Queens

Citi Field opened in 2009, replacing the legendary Shea Stadium. Built with a nod to Ebbets Field, it's widely considered one of the most beautiful ballparks in baseball.

From the Jackie Robinson Rotunda at the entrance to the big apple in left-center, everything about Citi Field is New York. On a warm summer night with a full house, there's no place like it.
